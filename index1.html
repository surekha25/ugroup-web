About Unique Group

The Empowering Business Partner!

At Unique Group, we position ourselves as 'The Empowering Business Partner' for companies looking to scale with the right support. With over 20 years of hands-on experience, we’ve helped businesses across industries unlock incentives through DGFT advisory, industrial subsidy consulting, and GST refunds.

We are a team of seasoned professionals, from chartered accountants, Bankers to CHAs, who bring deep domain expertise and strong institutional relationships. This allows us to offer strategic, end-to-end support that’s both reliable and result driven.

In 2022, we transitioned into a Private Limited company, marking a significant milestone in our growth and reaffirming our commitment to excellence and integrity.

We believe that navigating policies and paperwork shouldn’t hold your business back. That’s why we’re here - to make it seamless, structured, and successful.

What We Stand For
Our Guiding Values

End to End Services

We believe that our job does not end at submitting the application to the Implementing agency. But it ends only after you receive the final and complete benefit of the respective scheme.

Attention to Details

Projects and applications undergo very keen scrutiny from the respective implementing agencies. Even a small missing detail can become a cause for rejection of benefit / subsidy. We ensure that we leave no reason for the Implementing Agencies to reject the application.

Long Term
Commitment

Most of the times subsidies and benefits are not done in a single payment, they are paid as per different milestones reached in the project. And projects take few years to complete. We will provide our support till the project is completed and the complete benefit is received.

Single Window

For making an application complete, one has to take help of various professionals like Chartered Accountant, Chartered Engineer, Banks, Lawyers etc., With our deeply networked community you can get all those services through us, easily, in a Single Window.
